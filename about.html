About us

Welcome to Siyas cookhouse an indian food blog on simple vegetrian and non-vegetarian recipes.


> ABOUT ME

I am Swati shekhar a mom of 2 young boys living in singapore.

Coming from a very traditional indian Family, I learnt cooking food from CLUNIARY EXPORT in our extended family.

After 2 decades of experiance in practical indian cooking, in 2012 I started this blog to share my recipes that are simple, quick, delicious and healthy.

Developing healthy & wight gaining recipes for babies is something i love the most & it gives me immense pleasure and happiness.



> ABOUT THE BLOG

What started as a small space to share baby food kid's recipes over the years has grown into a larger community for learning simple & healthy indian cooking. kudos to the Readers for making Culinary canvas one of the top indian Food blog



> FOCUS ON HEALTH

I am a health freak and a food freak. I believe in eating wholesome foods for a happy mind and healthy body. so you will find out the recipes. most of the everybody food recipes shared here call for ingredients that are rich, wholesome and nourshing to the body. Most of these are free from refined or processed ingredients.

I also teach How to cook Restaurant style and street foods in a healthy way without the use of harmful ingredients like artificial food colors, MSG, Vanspati, and other chemical additives. Most often i also try to minimise the use of refind flours and deep flying where ever possible.
