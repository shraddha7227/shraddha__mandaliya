EGGLESS CAKE WITH SIYAS COOKH

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white or apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the frosting (optional):

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 and 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Sambar Ingredients:

instruction:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch (20 cm) round cake pan or line it with parchment paper.In a mixing bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Sifting helps to remove lumps and aerate the dry ingredients.In another bowl, mix together the milk, vegetable oil (or melted coconut oil), vanilla extract, and white vinegar (or lemon juice). The vinegar or lemon juice will act as an egg substitute and help the cake rise. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan. Smooth the top with a spatula. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Allow the cake to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then remove it from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rack Once the cake is completely cool, you can frost it with your favorite frosting. Popular choices are chocolate ganache, buttercream, cream cheese frosting, or a simple dusting of powdered sugar.

To serve:

Slice and enjoy your delicious eggless cake!
