TUTI FUTTI CAKE WITH SIYAS COOK

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (plain or flavored)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup tutti frutti (candied fruit bits)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Extra tutti frutti for sprinkling on top

instruction:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch (20 cm) round cake pan or line it with parchment paper.In a mixing bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. Set this dry mixture aside.In another large mixing bowl, cream the softened butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. This can take about 2-3 minutes using a hand mixer or a stand mixer.To the creamed butter and sugar, add the yogurt, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combinedGradually add the dry flour mixture to the wet mixture and mix until you have a smooth batter.Gently fold in the tutti frutti into the cake batter. Reserve some for garnishing if desired.Pour the cake batter into the prepared cake pan. Spread it evenly. If you reserved some tutti frutti, sprinkle it on top of the batter for garnish. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Allow the cake to cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then remove it from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rackOnce the Tutti Frutti cake has cooled, slice it into pieces and serve. It's perfect with a cup of tea or coffee.

To serve:

Enjoy your homemade Tutti Frutti cake! Feel free to get creative with the colors and flavors of the tutti frutti you use to make the cake more visually appealing and flavorful.Enjoy!!
