CHOCOLATE COOKIE WITH SIYAS COOKH

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

instruction:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Cream Butter and Sugars: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy. This usually takes about 2-3 minutes with an electric mixer. Add Eggs and Vanilla: Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; stop as soon as there are no streaks of flour in the dough.

Gently fold in the semi-sweet chocolate chips until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough. Form Cookie Dough Balls: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart. You can also use a cookie scoop for even-sized cookies. Bake: Place the baking sheets in the preheated oven and bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cookies are set and the edges are slightly firm. The centers may still look slightly soft; they will continue to set as they cool. Cool: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy: Once the cookies are completely cooled, enjoy them with a glass of milk or your favorite beverage.

To serve:

These chocolate cookies are perfect for any occasion or simply when you're in the mood for a delicious treat. You can also customize them by adding nuts, white chocolate chips, or other mix-ins to suit your taste.
